Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game, this figure is the value you must reach in order to win.

There are four colored crystals below. Each crystal has its own value, clicking on each will reveal its value for this round.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random figure generated at the beginning of the game, you lose the game if your total score goes above this figure.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. A new game will begin automatically once the previous one ends, your total wins and losses will be tallied during your exploration.

Each time the game starts, every crystal will have new values generated automatically. Good luck adventurer!
